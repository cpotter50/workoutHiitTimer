3 timers at 2 seconds each.

From the range of 1 to 6 seconds:
	Just move down the timers as normal.
	
Once you get to 7 seconds:
	Return to the first timer and run it from 0.

From 8 to 12 seconds:
	Loop through the timers as you did the first time. 
	
	- - -	-	-	-		-	-	-	-		--	-	-	-		-	--	
	
	
When any play button is hit:
	Turn all play buttons into pause buttons. 
	
When any pause button is hit:
	Turn all play buttons into pause buttons.
			If the main timer was hit: Resume time.
			If a timer's play button was hit: Play from 00:00 + all time of timer's before played timer


	-	-	-	-	-	-	-	-	-	-	-	-	-	-	-	-	-	-
